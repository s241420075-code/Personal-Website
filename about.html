About Anan Humaiza

Background, experience, and personal story

Background

Anan Humaiza is a 66-year-old Chairman of the Recreation Club at Timah Tasoh Lake, Perlis. He lives in Arau, Perlis and has a deep appreciation for the peaceful environment, beautiful lake scenery, and natural landscape of Timah Tasoh Lake.

Experience

Anan Humaiza has experience assisting visitors, sharing information about the lake area, guiding tourists, and helping maintain a clean and welcoming environment. His daily work allows him to meet people from different places and introduce them to the beauty of Perlis.

Personal Story

His interest in tourism started when he saw how Timah Tasoh Lake attracted many visitors for sightseeing, photography, and family recreation. This inspired him to become more involved in promoting the place as one of Perlis meaningful natural attractions.
