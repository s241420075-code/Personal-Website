Role and Contribution

Supporting tourism and nature awareness in Timah Tasoh Lake

His Contribution

Job Role

Anan Humaiza works as a nature tourism guide at Timah Tasoh Lake. His role includes assisting visitors, sharing basic information, guiding tourists, and supporting a safe visitor experience.

Tourism Promotion

He contributes to Perlis tourism by introducing Timah Tasoh Lake as a beautiful destination for sightseeing, photography, family recreation, and nature appreciation.

Environmental Awareness

Anan Humaiza encourages visitors to keep the area clean and respect the natural environment. His effort helps protect the lake and maintain a positive image of Timah Tasoh.

“Protecting nature today creates a better experience for tomorrow.”
